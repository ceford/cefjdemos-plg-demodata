Scotland has had a turbulent history stretching from Roman times, when in 122 CE the Emperor Hadrian ordered the building of a wall across the north of England to mark the northern boundary of the Roman empire, to the late 17th century when the last pitched battle on British soil was fought at Culloden in 1746.

This site is a demonstration designed show some of the capabilities of the Joomla! Content Management System (CMS). It is not intended to be a source of information on Scottish battles.

For more information see the Wikipedia article on Inventory of Historic Battlefields in Scotland. There are 40 battlefields listed in the inventory with only a selection summarised here.
